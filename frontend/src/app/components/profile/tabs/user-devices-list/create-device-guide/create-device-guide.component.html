Create device register the device on your network

In order for your device to send data to my.corrhizal.net it must have an active Wi-Fi connection, you will now connect to your device and upload your network credentials such that it may connect to the Internet.

  • Set device into AP mode
  • Reboot device
  • Disconnect from your current Wi-Fi connection, and connect to mcn-wd1m-XXXX, where XXXX will be random characters
  • Go to your web-browser and go to http://mcn-wd1m-XXXX.local/
  • You will be prompted with a page where you can enter your network credentials
  • Click save
  • Un-plug device, set into Station mode and plug back in, device will now attempt to connect to your network
  • Re-connect back to your normal Wi-Fi station
